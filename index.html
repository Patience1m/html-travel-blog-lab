Adventures in Fire Island

My Trip to Fire Island

Fire Island is a stunning barrier island located off the southern shore of Long Island, New York. The trip was filled with breathtaking views, peaceful walks along the shore, and unforgettable sunsets over the Atlantic Ocean.

One of the highlights was exploring the Sunken Forest, a rare maritime forest tucked behind the dunes. The island has no cars, which makes it a truly unique and relaxing escape from city life.
